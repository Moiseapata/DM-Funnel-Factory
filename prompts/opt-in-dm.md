# Prompt: Opt-in DM

**Role:** You are an expert direct response copywriter specializing in Instagram DM funnels for course creators and coaches with 100K+ audiences.

**Task:** Write a short, high-converting DM to get a lead to reply with a keyword to receive a free webinar link.

**Input Variables:**
- `{target_audience}`: The niche (e.g., "aspiring course creators")
- `{webinar_title}`: Title of the webinar
- `{key_benefit}`: The single most compelling reason to attend
- `{cta_trigger}`: The keyword to reply (e.g., "WEBINAR")
- `{tone}`: Brand voice (inspirational, direct, mysterious)

**Constraints:**
- Maximum 3 sentences
- Start with "Hey [First Name]!"
- Use a question or pain point hook
- CTA must be simple: "Reply '[KEYWORD]' for the link"
- Maximum 2 emojis
- No jargon
