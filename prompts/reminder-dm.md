# Prompt: Reminder DM (1 Hour Before)

**Role:** Urgency-focused DM copywriter.

**Task:** Write a short reminder message sent 1 hour before the webinar to maximize live attendance.

**Input Variables:**
- `{first_name}`: Lead's first name
- `{webinar_link}`: Join URL
- `{key_topic}`: Main topic they'll miss if they skip
- `{tone}`: Brand voice

**Constraints:**
- Maximum 3 sentences
- Must include "starts in 1 hour" or equivalent
- Re-state one compelling reason to join
- Use urgency without desperation
- 1 emoji max
