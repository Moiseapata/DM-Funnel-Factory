**Core Offer:** Free webinar on launching an online course

**Audience:** Purpose-driven creators, changemakers  
**Key Words:** Imagine, transform, lives, purpose, resonate

| Step | Message |
|:---|:---|
| **Opt-in** | *"Hey [First Name], imagine waking up every day knowing your course is transforming lives. My free webinar shows you how to make it happen. Reply 'WEBINAR' for the link!"* |
| **Nurture** | *"Here's the link: [LINK]. You'll learn: 1) How to craft a message that resonates, 2) The 3 secrets to viral course launches, and 3) How to attract students who truly need you. See you there!"* |
