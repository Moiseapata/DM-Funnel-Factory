# 🚀 The Complete DM Funnel Guide

## 4-Step DM Sequence for Webinar Promotion

### Step 1 – Opt-in DM
**Type:** Cold outreach or warm list activation  
**Goal:** Get the reply trigger word  
**KPI Target:** 20% response rate

**Structure:**
- Greeting with first name
- Call out a specific pain point
- Promise the solution via the webinar
- Frictionless CTA: "Reply [KEYWORD] for the link"

**Example (Amy Porterfield):**
> *"Hey [First Name]! 👋 Want to turn your expertise into a profitable online course—but not sure where to start? My free webinar shows you the exact steps to launch in 30 days. Reply 'WEBINAR' for the link!"*

---

### Step 2 – Nurture DM
**Type:** Immediate follow-up after opt-in  
**Goal:** Deliver the link and sell the promise  
**KPI Target:** 15% signup rate

**Structure:**
- Acknowledge their reply
- Deliver the link with date/time
- List 3 specific, irresistible benefits
- Friendly sign-off

**Example (Amy Porterfield):**
> *"Here's the link: [LINK]. The webinar is on June 10 at 2 PM EST. You'll learn: 1) How to validate your course idea, 2) The 3-step launch framework, and 3) How to attract your first students. See you there!"*

---

### Step 3 – Reminder DM
**Type:** 1-hour-before urgency trigger  
**Goal:** Maximize live attendance  
**KPI Target:** 30% attendance rate

**Structure:**
- Urgency hook ("starts in 1 hour")
- Re-link to join
- Re-state the main promise
- Urgency close ("Don't miss it")

**Example (Amy Porterfield):**
> *"Hey [First Name], the webinar starts in 1 hour! 🚀 Join here: [LINK]. We'll cover how to launch without overwhelm. Don't miss it!"*

---

### Step 4 – Sales DM
**Type:** Post-webinar value bridge  
**Goal:** Book a sales call or direct purchase  
**KPI Target:** 5% conversion rate

**Structure:**
- Thank them for attending
- Bridge the gap: webinar value → paid solution
- Name the program
- Soft CTA: "Reply 'YES' for a call"

**Example (Amy Porterfield):**
> *"Thanks for joining! If you're ready to launch your course without the guesswork, my program Course Launch Lab can help. Want to chat? Reply 'YES' for a call."*
